The First Project

Con Mua Ngang Qua

Son Tung M-TP's first song was the song "Con Mua Ngang Qua" for the Over Band and Young Pilots groups. He shared the song on the online music website Zing MP3 in August 2011. Within just 2 months after its release, the song achieved 1.7 million online streams. The success of "Con Mua Ngang Qua" has far exceeded Tung's expectations. Tung's name became widely known from here.

Breakthrough & Controversy over Copyright Issues

Em Cua Ngay Hom Qua

"Em cua ngay hom qua" launched the singer's mainstream career and gained more than 100 million streams within 3 months on Zing MP3. The music program Favorite Song voted this song as "Song of the Month" in February 2014. However, "Em cua ngay hom qua", "Con mua ngang qua" and many other songs by Tung was accused of plagiarism.

OST for the film "Chang trai nam ay"

Chac Ai Do Se Ve
Khong Phai Dang Vua Dau

In June 2014, there was news that the male singer would star in "Chang trai nam ay", a theatrical film inspired by the life of the late male singer Wanbi Tuan Anh. The film's soundtrack includes two successful singles by Son Tung "Chac ai đo se ve" and "Khong phai dang vua đau".

From hardships to the first accomplishment

Am Tham Ben Em

Son Tung released "Silent With You" in the form of a live performance with the band and streamed live through his YouTube channel. Although the records had modest commercial success compared to his previous songs, "Am tham ben em" still won the Green Wave Award in the Single of the Year category.

First Collaborations

Buong Doi Tay Nhau Ra
Mot Nam Moi Binh An

In December, Tung released the single "Buong doi tay nhau ra", marking his first collaboration with actress Chi Pu. On January 24, 2016, Son Tung M-TP collaborated with Chi Pu, Ho Ngoc Ha and Toc Tien to release the music video "Mot nam moi binh an", this is the second promotional single in his career, marking Tung becoming Oppo's brand ambassador in Vietnam.

First-hand Albums

Lac Troi
Noi Nay Co Anh

On January 1, 2017, Son Tung M-TP released the music video for the lead single taken from his debut compilation album, m-tp M-TP (2017), titled "Lac troi". This is the first song Tung released while working at M-TP Entertainment. On February 14, 2017, to celebrate Valentine's Day, Son Tung M-TP released the second single from the album called "Noi nay co anh" with many scenes filmed in Korea.

First Ever Autobiography

Cham Toi Giac Mo

On September 30, 2017, Son Tung released his autobiography "Touching the Dream", selling 10,000 copies in just 2 days. Son Tung said he had been writing this autobiography for a long time with the purpose of inspiring young people, encouraging them to dare to think and do to pursue their dreams.
Son Tung shared, "The autobiography "Cham toi giac mo" simply wants to share with you Tung's personal perspective, how Tung himself lives, works, studies, and strives to conquer his passion."

Reaching Out to The World

Hay Trao Cho Anh -
Give It To Me

On the night of June 28, Son Tung M-TP posted a 27-second trailer of "Hay trao cho anh" on his YouTube channel. In this new music product, with the goal of reaching the American and European music markets, he collaborated with American rapper Snoop Dogg and American actress & singer Madison Beer. The song has created many great impacts and continuously set a series of Vietnamese and world records, highly appreciated by the media.

Keep Upgrading

Chung Ta Cua Hien Tai
Muon Roi Ma Sao Con

On December 20, 2020, Son Tung M-TP announced the release of the music video "We are now" - the single that paved the way for his first Studio album - along with the appearance of Hai Tu, exclusive actress of
M-TP Entertainment. After just less than half a year, he continued to release "It's late but why are you still there" on April 29, 2021. The song is loved by many audiences because of its catchy and memorable melody.

Try Something New?

There's No One at All
Making My Way

On April 12, 2022, Son Tung M-TP officially posted a poster related to the first English song of his career titled "There's No One at All". For the first time, Son Tung M-TP experimented with composing the hip-hop mixed rock genre, singing entirely in English. Son Tung M-TP announced the release of his second English single titled "Making My Way" on April 25, 2023, the song was released on May 5, 2023.

Latest Release

Chung Ta Cua Tuong Lai

At exactly 0:00 on March 8, the original Thai Binh singer released the MV for the song We of the Future, continuing the story four years ago in the MV We of the Present. In this music video, Son Tung tries to find the silhouette of the female lead (played by Hai Tu) in different dimensions. Both have feelings in those four worlds, but none of them have a complete ending.
