About Hi, I'm Son Tung M-TP!

"Son Tung M-TP: Journey from Thai Binh to the Pinnacle of Vietnamese Music"

Nguyen Thanh Tung, born on July 5, 1994, is a Vietnamese singer-songwriter, rapper, and actor professionally known as Son Tung M-TP. Hailing from the city of Thai Binh, young Tung frequented the Thai Binh Children's Palace to sing and learned to play the organ. Later, he formed a band called Over Band with his classmates and began composing and uploading their songs to a music website named LadyKillah. Tung's fame skyrocketed with the release of his singles "Con mua ngang qua" and "Em cua ngay hom qua," marking the beginning of his successful career.

Son Tung M-TP: An Artistic Journey and Multifaceted Career

A Record Label and Production Company:

Son Tung M-TP spent over four years associated with the record labels Van Production and WePro Entertainment before establishing his own label, M-TP Entertainment, in late 2016. This move marked a significant step in his journey towards independent artistic control and creative freedom.

M-TP Ambition Tour:

In late 2015, Son Tung M-TP embarked on his first concert tour, aptly named "M-TP Ambition - The First Flight." The tour spanned across various cities in Vietnam, showcasing his rising popularity and captivating stage presence.

Acting Debut and Film Recognition:

Son Tung M-TP ventured into the world of acting with his debut role in the 2014 film "Chang trai nam ay." His portrayal of the protagonist earned him a Golden Kite Award for Promising Young Actor, demonstrating his versatility as an artist.

The Remix and Musical Collaborations:

In 2015, Son Tung M-TP expanded his musical reach by participating as a contestant in the popular music program "The Remix" This platform provided him with opportunities to collaborate with established musicians and further showcase his musical talents.

Chuyen đi cua thanh xuan: A Short Film Collaboration:

In July 2018, Son Tung M-TP took on a guest role in the short film "Chuyen đi cua thanh xuan," directed by Nguyen Quang Dung and produced in collaboration with Biti's Hunter. This project showcased his willingness to explore different creative avenues beyond music.

Sky Tour Documentary: A Glimpse Behind the Scenes:

Son Tung M-TP's first documentary film, "Sky Tour," premiered in 2020. This intimate portrayal delved into the behind-the-scenes process of creating and executing his "Sky Tour" concert, offering fans a deeper understanding of his artistic vision and dedication to his craft.

A Multifaceted Artist and Industry Leader:

Son Tung M-TP's journey extends beyond music, encompassing acting, filmmaking, and business ventures. His multifaceted talents and entrepreneurial spirit have solidified his position as a leading figure in the Vietnamese entertainment industry. He continues to inspire and captivate audiences with his innovative approach to music, fashion, and self-expression.

Son Tung M-TP: A Vpop Legend and Global Breakout Star

Proclaimed as the "Prince of Vpop," Son Tung M-TP has garnered acclaim from music journalists, critics, and authors as one of the most successful Vpop artists of our time. Throughout his illustrious career, Tung has amassed an impressive collection of both domestic and international accolades, including: 1 Devotion Music Awards, 1 MTV Europe Music Award, 1 Mnet Asian Music Award, 7 Green Wave Awards, ranked in Forbes Vietnam's 30 Under 30 list. Son Tung MTP is the only vietnamese artist who is ranked among the 5 standard billboards of the Billboard journal, he also won twice the LyricFind Global Chart and often appeared on the Vietnam Hot 100 and Top Vietnamese Songs.

Son Tung M-TP: A Multifaceted Talent and Style Icon

Beyond his captivating music, Son Tung M-TP stands as a gifted music producer, seamlessly blending modern and traditional elements with remarkable creativity. His involvement extends beyond the recording booth, actively participating in the songwriting and production process, resulting in unique masterpieces that have taken the Vietnamese music scene by storm.

Son Tung's artistic prowess extends to the silver screen, where he has captivated audiences with his impressive performances in various roles, ranging from feature films to television projects. His natural acting abilities and versatility have undoubtedly contributed to the allure of the productions he has graced.

From a young boy with a musical dream to a prominent figure in the entertainment industry, Son Tung M-TP has consistently proven his unwavering determination, relentless effort, and exceptional talent. With millions of devoted fans and recognition from both the artistic community and the general public, he stands as one of the most prominent figures not only in Vietnam but also on the international stage.
